About Royse City Junk Removal

Royse City's locally owned, full-service junk removal company — proudly serving our community as a division of I30 Builders.

Who We Are

Royse City Junk Removal has been serving Northeast Texas since 2017. We're a division of I30 Builders, a construction and property services company headquartered in Caddo Mills, TX, with a satellite office right here in Royse City. We're locally owned, licensed, and fully insured.

We started because our community needed a junk removal company that shows up on time, gives honest prices, and treats people right. Whether it's a single piece of furniture or a full estate cleanout, we handle every job with the same care and professionalism.

When you call us, you're hiring your neighbors — not a franchise. We live and work in the same communities we serve across Hunt County, Rockwall County, and the surrounding areas.

"We live here. This is our community. We treat every job like we're working for a neighbor — because we are."
