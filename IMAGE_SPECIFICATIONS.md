## 📋 IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS BY PLATFORM

### Google Ads (Responsive Search Ads)

✅ **Recommended Sizes:**
- 1200 x 628 px (1.91:1) - PRIMARY
- 1200 x 1200 px (1:1) - SECONDARY

✅ **Requirements:**
- Format: JPG or PNG
- NO text overlays
