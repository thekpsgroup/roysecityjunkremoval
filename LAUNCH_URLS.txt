## NOTES

- Replace `junk_removal` with specific campaign name (furniture_removal, same_day, etc.)
- utm_source should match the platform name
- utm_medium should stay as `cpc` for paid ads
- The page captures gclid, msclkid, and fbclid click IDs automatically
- Old URLs (/ads-google, /ads-microsoft, /free-quote) 301 redirect to /quote
